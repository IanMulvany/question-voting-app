Welcome to the Idea Voting App!

This application allows you to submit your ideas and questions, vote on them, and manage them through an admin interface.

  • Submit Questions: Share your thoughts or questions with others.
  • Vote: Participate by voting on the ideas you find most interesting or important.
  • Admin: Manage the submitted ideas and questions.
  • View Questions: See all submitted questions and ideas.

Use the navigation links above to explore the different features of the app.
